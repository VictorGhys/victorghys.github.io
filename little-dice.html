Little Dice

Little Dice is a cute 2D puzzle game. We gathered together a team of 5 people. For programming Gamer Hangout and me. For art Jest Quest and Lilly Martin. And for sound Thomas Thoreau.

In the game you need to put the abilities you are given in the right slots, so you can traverse the level and reach the end. I mostly worked on the ability system, made the slots and dragging system and the jump ability.

This was the most fun I had in a game jam thusfar. We came up with an original concept and everyone did an amazing job in the execution. We also received lots of positive comments and feedback on the game.

The game was made for the GMTK game jam in 48 hours. The theme was "Roll of the Dice". The game was 195th overall out of 6168 entries, which is in the top 3%. Made in Unity.
