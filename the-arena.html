The Arena

An environment of a sci-fi arena made for the course Environments 1. The only way that I could make such a big level in a short time is with the modular assets pack that I made. Also almost the whole environment uses a single trim texture. We learned to work smart and use our time efficiently. And learned to make reusable parameterized shaders.

Made in Unreal Engine 4. 3D models are made in 3ds Max. Texture made in Photoshop.
